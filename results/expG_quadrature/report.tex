\title{\vspace{-2em}\textbf{Experiment G --- Quadrature Speed Verification}}
\author{}
\date{}

\begin{document}
\maketitle
\vspace{-3em}

Verifies that DH 2$\times$ is really $+45\%$ slower than DH default,
and that GL is really free.

\section*{Setup}

\begin{itemize}\setlength\itemsep{0pt}
\item \textbf{GPU}: NVIDIA A100-40GB (Perlmutter), confirmed idle (\texttt{nvidia-smi}).
\item \textbf{Model}: real \texttt{EquiformerV2Backbone} from fairchem, OC20-scale config:
  12 layers, 128 channels, lmax$=$6, mmax$=$2 $\rightarrow$ 24 \texttt{S2Activation} modules.
\item \textbf{Data}: 3 QM9 batches (batch$=$8) pre-fetched \emph{once} and reused for every config.
\end{itemize}

\begin{center}
\small
\begin{tabular}{llcr}
\toprule
Config       & Method                              & Grid (lat $\times$ lon) & Total pts \\
\midrule
DH default   & e3nn equiangular                    & 14 $\times$ 5  & 70 \\
DH 2$\times$ & DH with \texttt{resolution=4(L+1)}  & 28 $\times$ 28 & 784 \\
GL match-DH  & custom \texttt{CustomSO3Grid}       & 14 $\times$ 13 & 182 \\
GL 2$\times$ & custom \texttt{CustomSO3Grid}       & 14 $\times$ 28 & 392 \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{center}

\section*{Protocol (per config, per run)}

\begin{enumerate}\setlength\itemsep{0pt}
\item Build a fresh model, patch SiLU $+$ (optionally) swap \texttt{SO3\_Grid} to GL.
\item \textbf{20 warmup} forward passes.
\item \textbf{30 measured} forward passes, each wrapped in \texttt{torch.cuda.synchronize()}.
\item \texttt{del model; cuda.empty\_cache()} before next config.
\end{enumerate}

\section*{Controls}

\begin{center}
\small
\begin{tabular}{ll}
\toprule
Confound & Mitigation \\
\midrule
Async kernels        & \texttt{cuda.synchronize()} around every iter \\
Cold start           & 20-iter warmup \\
Run-to-run drift     & 3 independent sweeps \\
Ordering effects     & Forward / reverse / shuffled orderings \\
Different inputs     & Same 3 batches reused for all configs \\
Memory carryover     & \texttt{del model + empty\_cache} between configs \\
Other GPU users      & Verified \texttt{nvidia-smi --query-compute-apps} empty \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{center}

\section*{Results (3 runs $\times$ 30 iters $=$ 90 measurements per config)}

\begin{center}
\small
\begin{tabular}{lccl}
\toprule
Config & Mean $\pm$ 95\% CI (ms) & $\Delta$ vs DH default & Significance \\
\midrule
DH default       & $111.83 \pm 0.79$ & ---                       & --- \\
\textbf{DH 2$\times$}    & $\mathbf{162.73 \pm 1.23}$ & $\mathbf{+50.90~ms~(+45.5\%)}$ & $\mathbf{35\times}$ \textbf{CI} \\
GL match-DH      & $112.23 \pm 0.75$ & $+0.40$~ms ($+0.4\%$)     & not sig.\ \\
GL 2$\times$     & $111.34 \pm 0.76$ & $-0.49$~ms ($-0.4\%$)     & not sig.\ \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{center}

DH 2$\times$ measured 162.60 / 162.35 / 163.22 ms across the three orderings ---
position-independent. Run-to-run std of the means is 0.36 ms.

\section*{Sanity check}

S2-act kernel alone: DH default 0.34 ms, DH 2$\times$ 2.36 ms (per call).
24 modules $\times$ ($2.36 - 0.34$) $=$ \textbf{48.5 ms predicted} vs
\textbf{50.9 ms observed}. The 2.4 ms gap is kernel-launch overhead.

\section*{Reproduce}

PYTORCH_CUDA_ALLOC_CONF=expandable_segments:True \

\section*{Takeaway}

GL 2$\times$ delivers DH 2$\times$'s equivariance at DH default's
wall-clock cost. Switching from DH default to DH 2$\times$ costs
$+51$ ms/forward; switching to GL is free. The advantage scales with
model size (negligible at QM9 scale, $+45\%$ at OC20 scale).
